    FAQ

    Special Stars Organization is a non-profit organization that serves children with special needs and their families. We offer them the essential support and services they need to improve their quality of life. We also strive to promote awareness and advocacy for children with special needs and their rights in society.

    Our organization is unique in that we work with children with special needs socially and support them in their own fields while most organizations focus on donation only. As we are starting our journey, our specialties will be revealed in the future. Also our organization's team has a great personality and they are very friendly.

    Our first contribution was to provide some books, a color pencil box to a girl with special needs who loves to draw, and a cricket set to a 10-year-old youngster who enjoys playing with his friends, but he does not have any of the necessary equipment. We are very happy to be able to help them. We will continue to help children with special needs in the future.
