    • About Special Stars

    Special Stars is a compassionate and dedicated organization established to bring positive change and support to individuals who face challenges related to disabilities and autism. Founded with the vision of empowering abilities and embracing dreams, Special Stars strives to create a more inclusive and understanding society, where every individual can thrive and achieve their full potential.
