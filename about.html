    • About Special Stars

    Special Stars Organization is a non-profit organization that aims to help children with special needs. We are a group of people who are passionate about helping children with special needs and their families. We believe that every child deserves a chance to live a happy and fulfilling life.

    We provide support to families who have children with special needs by providing them with the resources they need to care for their child. Our goal is to make sure that every child has access to the care they need so they can grow up healthy and happy.
